About us

Mumble Sound is an event, record label and collective that delivers the weird and wonderful.
‍
Fusing interactive content with spectacular immersive performers in addition to our carefully selected resident DJ’s and surprise special guests. Offering a cross section of House, Funk and Disco.
‍
It's the perfect night for clubbers to experience a more relaxed side of electronic music at some of the south coasts best event spaces and nightclubs.
